Let's talk about playing guitar

Beginning steps and recomendations

1/14/2024



I consider myself an avid, intermediate guitarist. I have four guitars of varying purposes, and I enjoy playing each of them every day. I find them relaxing to play, and I really only perform them for my own happiness. I am self-taught to a large degree, and I am responsible (and motivated) for my own learning.

One pattern I notice in guitarists that fall out of love for their instrument is a lack of motivation. These guitarists feel that they experience too much pressure to study advanced skills and concepts, so they drift away from playing. Advanced skills and concepts, such as scales and music theory, are great to know but aren't necessary to build confidence in an instrument. The advice I can give to beginning guitarists is to just play songs that they like and tinker around on their guitar, and really follow what they enjoy doing. When they feel ready to 'level up' their skills, they should definitely seek more advanced topics, but not necessarily before then.
