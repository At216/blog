Let's talk about web advertisements

Should there be boundaries?

1/16/2024



  We encounter internet advertisements almost every time we visit a website. Internet advertisements are great for the website owner, as they can make revenue from them. However, some websites are so chock-full of advertisements that they experience significant delays in loading and are difficult to use. Some websites, such as Mathisfun.com, practice a solution to intrusive advertisements that I appreciate (and believe many others appreciate also).

  When browsing Mathisfun.com, I came across one singular advertisement on each page. Each advertisement has a button beside it that minimizes it. In addition, Mathisfun.com has a button that hides all advertisiments across all pages for a period of time. According to the developer,

Ads can sometimes be distracting, so you can hide them for 36 hours (yes they come back, but you can hide them again).

In other words, you have the choice to opt out of advertisements. We already are given choices on most websites to opt out of data tracking out of reasons for courtesy (and frankly, sometimes legality). Website developers should take the next step, and let us choose our browsing experience in full — without internet advertisements
